Our Success Story

CEDCOSS believes in democratising the e -commerce for small, medium and big businesses alike. Through its e-commerce web development arm, Cedcommerce, the company offers tailor-made solutions that make it easy for merchants to start, grow and expand their online shops. The e-commerce market offers a gargantuan opportunity with approximated sales of $2,352 billion sales worldwide in 2017 (according to Statista). Started in 2010, the company now has more than 100 employees. The company is the member of NASSCOM and ISO 9001:2008 certified. The company has developed more than 100 products for more than 15 e-commerce Frameworks including Magento,WooCommerce , Shopify.
